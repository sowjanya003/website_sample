About Us

Located in the peaceful village of Burrilanka in East Godavari District, SKM EM School is a private, government-recognized institution offering quality education from nursery to 7th class. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive learning environment that helps children grow academically and personally. We focus on sparking a love for learning and developing critical thinking, creativity, and curiosity in every student.

Affordable Quality Education

We pride ourselves on being a budget-friendly institution, offering low fees without compromising on the quality of education. We believe that every child deserves access to excellent education regardless of their financial background. Our mission is to make quality education accessible to all families in our community.

Facilities and Environment

We offers a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for our students. Our classrooms are well-equipped with the necessary learning resources, and we maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention for each child. We are continuously working to improve our facilities and provide a conducive atmosphere for learning and growth.

Community and Values

We are proud to be a part of the vibrant Burrilanka community. Our school promotes values such as respect, responsibility, and integrity. We strive to instill these values in our students, preparing them to be responsible citizens and future leaders.
